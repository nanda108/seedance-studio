## СТИЛИ-ПРЕСЕТЫ

### CINEMATIC PHOTOREALISTIC (базовый)
"Photorealistic. ARRI Alexa. 35mm Master Prime. Professional color grading. Volumetric lighting. Film grain subtle 35mm. 8K sharpness. Cinematic 2.39:1."

### NEO-NOIR (Deakins / Willis)
"Deep shadow pools. Single hard key light at 45 degrees. Wet reflective surfaces — rain on pavement, neon in puddles. Desaturated mid-tones, only neon colors pop. High contrast 8:1. Charcoal, amber, electric blue palette."

### GOLDEN HOUR (Deakins в Sicario)
"Shot at civil twilight. Sky transitions orange to midnight blue simultaneously. Long shadows, low angle sun. Warm foreground, cold sky. Maximum color contrast."

### COLD INDUSTRIAL / DIGITAL
"Cold blue-grey grade. Desaturated skin tones. Fluorescent practicals as key. Clean digital, no grain. Slate grey, powder blue palette. 85mm+, shallow DOF on faces."

### NATURALISTIC / DOCUMENTARY (Lubezki)
"Natural light exclusively. No color grade. Slight overexposure in highlights acceptable. Film grain from pushed ISO. Handheld. Raw earth tones, grey sky, flame orange."

### LUXURY / COMMERCIAL (Apple, Chanel, Dior)
"Impeccably soft key — large diffused source. Rim light separating subject. Cream, champagne, midnight black — 2-3 colors max. 85-105mm. Background perfectly out of focus. Surface reflections: glass, steel, water, silk."

### HORROR / PSYCHOLOGICAL (Willis + Kubrick)
"Top-down lighting — eyes in deep shadow. Practical sources only. 12:1 contrast. Desaturated green or cold white. Symmetrical framing. Static camera. Long takes."

### UGC / AUTHENTIC PHONE
"Shot on iPhone — no color grade, no stabilization. Auto-exposure flickering. Natural grain. Slight motion blur. No professional lighting. Real. Unpolished."

### MUSIC VIDEO — DARK EDITORIAL
"Deep desaturation — near monochrome. Blacks crushed to zero. Single fluorescent rim from behind. High contrast, no fill. Heavy film grain. Cut on beat."

### CGI / GAME TRAILER
"Cinematic video game trailer. Photorealistic CGI. Unreal Engine 5 quality. Particle effects: dust, smoke, sparks. God rays through atmosphere."

### SOVIET / POST-SOVIET
"Cyrillic signage everywhere. No English text. Brutalist Soviet architecture. Desaturated cold palette. Grey sky."
